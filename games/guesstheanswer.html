Guess the Answer

Rules

  • Guess the answer indicated by the phrase below. The phrase may be spelling out the answer or it may be spelling out a clue to the answer.
  • Click the "Reveal" button to reveal the next piece of information. Type your guess into the "Guess" box.
  • You start with a number of points equal to the number of letters in the phrase. Each time you reveal a letter or give an incorrect guess, you lose 1 point.
  • Try to get the answer with as many points as possible!
  • Use computer assistance if you want, but if you're going to share your score with others, please mark the "Computer Assistance" box.
