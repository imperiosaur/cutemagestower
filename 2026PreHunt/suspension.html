Suspension


I keep seeing the same portly gentleman in different places all over the world.

I’ve seen him…

  • … leaving a pet shop with terriers in California.
  • … crossing a Quebec staircase, right at the top.
  • … passing a display of paintings in a small Vermont village, near the parked limousine of an old art lover.
  • … drinking champagne at a party in a Rio mansion.
  • … in a newspaper ad, stranded in the Atlantic.
  • … riding a bus with a very handsome man in the Riviera one day.
  • … from behind, watching an acrobatic troupe in the Morocco marketplace, wearing a suit and puIng his hands into his pockets.
  • … in Sydney, standing with two other men on the steps of a government building.
  • … just outside of a Phoenix office.
  • … in a Manhattan apartment, where he was winding a clock.
  • … bouncing a child on his knee in a hotel in Copenhagen.
  • … carrying a trumpet across a Frisco street.
  • … at a flat in London, staring out from a photograph of a reunion dinner.
  • … trying to board at the railway station in a town called Metcalf, with a double bass fiddle.
  • … just missing a green city bus in Manhattan, where the doors unceremoniously closed in his face.
