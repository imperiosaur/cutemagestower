Flying Saucer


On no! The Xyrmbats' flying saucer has been boarded by the villainous Oombs! Can Captain Xigna keep her position in the center seat?

The Xyrmbats are armed with Deceptor missiles; a Deceptor missile aimed in one direction follows a looping course, ultimately hitting a target 90Â° from the direction of aim. (In particulary, if a Deceptor missile is aimed at a given target, it will never hit that target.) The Oombs are armed with Straightbolt missiles, which fly straight. (Thus, if a Straightbolt missile is aimed at a target, it wil always hit that target.)
  1. Every missile was aimed north, south, east, or west (but not necessarily aimed at anyone).
  2. Every missile fired hit someone adjacent to the firer.
  3. No two missiles fired by the same creature followed the same trajectory.
  4. Every Xyrmbat who fired aimed all her missiles in the same direction.
  5. Every Xyrmbat who fired fired a different number of missiles.
  6. Every Oomb further east than Captain Xigna got hit exactly once.
  7. Captain Xigna fired exactly one missile; it was left-handed.
  8. No Oomb who was aimed at fired.
  9. Every Oomb who was not aimed at fired exactly one more missile in a north-south direction than in an east-west direction.
  10. Exactly four missiles were aimed north.
  11. The difference between the number of missiles a creature fired and got hit with was never exactly two.
